JavaScript for Data Science

Update: JavaScript, node and browsers seem have changed since the book was written. This has made progress difficult at times, and I have not been able to complete the capstone project or the testing chapter to a level I am happy with. Although, this is in itself may be an important lesson about web development. It's a fast-paced crazy field, and not one to be taken on lightly.
