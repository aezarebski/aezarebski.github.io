Welcome

Hello, I am a Lecturer in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Melbourne. I am interested in the application of mathematics, statistics and computation to a range of problems in infectious diseases, epidemiology and public health.

If you're interested in my work please contact me at azarebski (at) unimelb.edu.au
