Notes

  • 501.V2 variant (also known as The South African Variant and B.1.351) has important mutations: N501Y, K417N, and E484K. The Oxford-AstraZeneca vaccine appears to have reduced efficacy against this variant.
  • E484K is a mutation which appears to assist the virus with escaping antibodies developed in response to previous forms.
